Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

1. General Provisions

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and other applicable EU laws.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Name, surname, email address, phone number
  • Login data (username, password)
  • Payment information (if you make transactions)
  • IP address, browser data, cookies, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data (logs, activity history)
  • Other information you voluntarily provide

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • User registration and identification
  • Providing access to the service and its features
  • Processing payments and ensuring transaction security
  • Improving service quality, analytics, and statistics
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Communicating with users (email, messages)
  • AML/KYC compliance (anti-money laundering and customer identification)
  • Consumer rights protection compliance

4. Data Storage and Protection

Your data is stored on secure servers within the EU. We use modern technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. Only authorized personnel have access to personal data.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or necessary to provide the service (e.g., payment providers, auditors, government authorities as required by law).

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectify inaccuracies
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., DPA in your EU country or the Ombudsman in Ukraine)

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve site performance, analytics, personalization, and marketing. You can manage your cookie settings in your browser. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Changes to the Policy

We may periodically update this policy. The current version is always available on this page. If changes are significant, we will notify you additionally (e.g., by email or in-service message).

9. Contacts
